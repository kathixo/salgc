SALGC 5-Day SETA-Accredited Programs

Practical, accredited skills training designed to build Smart-Villages, create employment, and foster sustainable self-reliance across South Africa.

Renewable Solar Energy Training

Hands-on training to prepare communities for sustainable solar installations, mitigating loadshedding and opening new green-energy income streams.

  • 5-Day theory and practical program
  • Includes solar panel installation & maintenance
  • Graduation after 6 months post-training mentorship

Apiculture (Bee-Farming)

Bee-farming requires minimal capital and offers diverse income streams — honey, candles, pollination, and bee-removal services.

  • Understanding Bee-Farming (Removals)
  • Honey Production & Processing
  • Bee-Farming Economy & Profit Models
  • Products & Service Development

Cuniculture (Rabbit-Farming)

Low-cost entry farming ideal for youth and women entrepreneurs. Teaches sustainable production for lean meat, fur, and manure.

  • Modern rabbit husbandry
  • Breeding & disease control
  • Processing & marketing techniques
  • AgriSETA-aligned certification

Plant Farming (Crop Production)

Supports emerging farmers with essential crop production knowledge to ensure food security, employment, and income generation.

  • Soil & pest management
  • Sustainable farming practices
  • Market readiness and business skills

Mixed Farming (Crop & Livestock Integration)

Teaches integration of crop and livestock systems for better resource use, resilience, and diversified income.

  • Efficient land & feed management
  • Farm business administration
  • Environmental sustainability

Reviving human conscience and giving back hope to communities nationwide.
