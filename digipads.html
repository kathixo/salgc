Organic Dignity Pads

SALGC proudly promotes South African-made Organic Dignity Pads to ensure no girl misses school because of menstruation. Safe, breathable, and chemical-free.

  • Prevents itchiness, dryness, and fibroids
  • Reduces risk of cervical cancer
  • Eco-friendly and proudly South African
  • Affordable & accessible to schools & communities

Why Organic Matters

Most commercial sanitary pads contain plastics, bleaches, and synthetic fragrances that can cause irritation and long-term health issues. Our organic pads are made with natural cotton fibers and breathable layers that protect women’s health and comfort.

By choosing Organic Dignity Pads, you not only care for your body but also help young girls regain their confidence and education.
